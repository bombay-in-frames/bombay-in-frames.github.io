Harshita Sonkar

Born in India, I have been writing poems since early childhood. My passion for art extends to social media, where I frequently share my work. Born from personal experiences and a deep empathy for those navigating heartbreak, the poems offer solace and a shared language of emotions. I am trying to delve into the complexities of love's aftermath, crafting a bridge of understanding for those grappling with loss and the path towards healing.
